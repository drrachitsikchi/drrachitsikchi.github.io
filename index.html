About the doctor

Patient-centered psychiatric care with attention to the whole person.

Dr. Rachit Sikchi is a dedicated psychiatrist and sexologist committed to compassionate, evidence-based mental health care. He diagnoses and treats a wide range of psychiatric conditions including depression, anxiety disorders, OCD, schizophrenia, bipolar disorder, stress-related disorders, addiction, and sleep problems.

His approach focuses on understanding each person's concerns and creating personalized treatment plans that may include medication management, counseling, and lifestyle interventions. He is also experienced in treating sexual health concerns such as erectile dysfunction, premature ejaculation, low sexual desire, and relationship-related intimacy issues.
